A Pathetic Life

Okaka is 25 and recently became a mother. She is currently on the verge of divorce.

Her husband is both verbally abusive and neglectful. After abandoning his parental responsibilities, he ceased all communication.

Despite everything, she struggles with the fact that she still, somehow, loves him. She is unable to take that final, necessary step toward filing for divorce.

The question remains: What direction will her life take next?
